QORMA

Qorma is a famous and delicious Pakistani dish made with meat, spices, and yogurt. Its rich and flavorful taste, creamy texture, and aromatic spices make it very popular among food lovers.

BIRYANI

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish, typically made with basmati rice, meat (such as chicken, beef, or mutton), and a blend of spices. It is often cooked with layers of fried onions, yogurt, and herbs, and is known for its rich taste and colorful appearance.

KABAB

Kabab is a savory dish made by grilling or skewering marinated meat, usually chicken, beef, or lamb, along with vegetables. The meat is seasoned with a mixture of spices, herbs, and sometimes yogurt, which tenderizes it and enhances the flavor.

KARAHI

Karahi is a traditional and flavorful dish from Pakistan, typically made with chicken, mutton, or beef, cooked in a wok-like vessel called a "karahi." The meat is cooked with tomatoes, green chilies, ginger, garlic, and a variety of aromatic spices, creating a rich, spicy, and savory gravy.

GAJAR KA HALWA

Gajar ka Halwa is a popular and delicious Indian and Pakistani dessert made from grated carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee. The grated carrots are slow-cooked in milk until tender, and then sugar and ghee are added to create a rich, sweet, and aromatic mixture. It is often garnished with nuts like almonds or pistachios, and sometimes flavored with cardamom for an extra touch of fragrance
